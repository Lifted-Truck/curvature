# Timbre palette — same engine, different corners of parameter space

Axes demonstrated: damping (T60/tilt), mallet, register, mode count,
geometry-as-harmonicity (elongated torus -> harmonic series), and
sustained excitation (crude Phase 2 bow preview).

- `wood_icosphere.wav` — T60 0.35s, steep tilt: marimba/woodblock family — damping alone leaves 'metal'
- `membrane_torus.wav` — low register, soft mallet, sub-second decay: tom/membrane family
- `gong_genus2.wav` — genus-2 density at 55 Hz with 14 s ring: dark gong/tam-tam wash
- `glass_icosphere_12modes.wav` — only 12 modes, high register: glass/chime/music-box — mode count as a simplicity knob
- `string_torus_8to1.wav` — 8:1 flat torus: lowest modes form an exact harmonic series -> plucked-string family, from geometry alone
- `drone_genus2_bowed.wav` — sustained excitation of the dense genus-2 spectrum: shimmer drone/pad (crude Phase 2 bow preview)
- `organ_torus_8to1_bowed.wav` — sustained harmonic-series torus: organ/bowed-string family
